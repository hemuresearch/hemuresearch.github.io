Short Bio

Tian Cheng is a postdoctoral researcher at Media Interaction Group in National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST), working with Dr Masataka Goto. She received her Ph.D. from Queen Mary University of London in 2016, supervised by Prof. Simon Dixon and Dr Matthias Mauch. Her research domain is Music Information Research, including piano transcription, beat tracking, structure analysis and music similarity.
